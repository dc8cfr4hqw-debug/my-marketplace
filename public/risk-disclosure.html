Risk Disclosure

Digital asset and market-based investing involves substantial risk and may result in loss of capital.

Market Volatility

Prices may move rapidly due to macro events, liquidity, sentiment, and policy changes.

Execution and Liquidity Risk

Orders may execute at different prices than expected, especially during high volatility.

Technology and Operational Risk

Outages, internet disruptions, and third-party service interruptions may impact access or execution.

Regulatory Risk

Local laws may change and affect product availability, restrictions, or reporting requirements.

User Responsibility

Only invest amounts you can afford to lose and evaluate diversification, time horizon, and risk tolerance.
